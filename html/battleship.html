How to Play


  • 1. Press the start button and select either the Classic mode and the advanced mode
  • 2. Then shuffle your ships onto the stragtey panel or arrange them yourself
  • 3. Then take turns with trying to sink your opponent's ships
  • 4. If you sink all of your opponents ships, and you will get points accoridngly and win!!!
  • You are allowed to quit at any point of the game
