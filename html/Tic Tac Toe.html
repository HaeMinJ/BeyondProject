UNBEATABLE Tic Tac Toe


How to play Tic Tac Toe. Rules and instructions. Tic Tac Toe is a paper and pencil game for a two-players. There is a 3x3 grid where you take turns marking the spaces in a 3x3 grid with either X’s or O’s. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a vertical, horizontal, or diagonal row wins the game. The game is over when a player succeeds in placing three marks in a row, or when all the spaces in the grid are marked. If the game ends without a win for any of the players - it is called a “tie”.
